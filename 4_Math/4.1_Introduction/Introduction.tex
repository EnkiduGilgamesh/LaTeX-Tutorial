        The Commutative law is
        $$
            a*b=b*a
        $$
        % or
        The Associative law is:
        \[
            (a*b)*c=a*(b*c)
        \]
        % or
        The Distributive law is:
        \begin{displaymath}
            a*(b+c)=a*b+a*c
        \end{displaymath}

            a+b=b+a \label{eq:commutative}
        \end{equation}
